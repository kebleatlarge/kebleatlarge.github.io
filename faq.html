Keble at Large is a student-run voluntary organisation dedicated to directly helping applicants from school with a limited history of successful Oxbridge applications in order to ensure that Oxbridge is open to people from all backgrounds.


Primarily, Keble at Large runs tours of the college and Q&A sessions with current undergraduates for potential applicants, busting myths regarding the application process and showing them what life at Oxford is really like. Keble at Large also runs an annual Alternative Open Day in the autumn to directly assist applicants with interview and aptitude test practice.


If you are an Oxford undergraduate, Keble At Large is always looking for volunteers to help run tours and advise potential applicants. Fill out a form or send an email here
If you are a potential applicant seeking assistance or a teacher looking to organise a tour, fill out a form here or email nicole.boothman@keble.ox.ac.uk directly.
