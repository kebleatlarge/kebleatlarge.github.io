What we do

Working with and funded through the college, the Keble at Large produces a wide-range of literature dedicated to providing as much information as possible to as many prospective students while keeping it friendly and easy to digest. The Society, alongside the college, also organises and conducts school visits with the aim to inform students and teachers alike of the real Oxford that we all know and love. So, whether you are a prospective student contemplating university, a teacher looking to encourage applications, or a Keblite keen to get involved, have a look around the website and see what we can do for you!
